Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines eggs, herbs, and chesse for a tasty meal with any number of fillings

    Preparation time

  • Total:Approximatly 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cookinf:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large egges
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil

Instructions

  1. Crack the egges into a bownl and beat them until the yolks and whites are combined. Season with salt and pepper
  2. Place a non stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter or oil.
  3. Once the butter has melted or the oil is hot, pour in the egges.
  4. Let the egges cook undestributed until they start to set arround the edges of the skillet
  5. Using a spatula, gently push one edge of the egge into the center of the skillet, tilting the pan so the uncooked egges flow in underneath.
  6. Repeat with the other edges, until there is no liquid left
  7. Your omelette is ready! You can add your favourite fillings now, or simply slide it into a plate and enjoy
